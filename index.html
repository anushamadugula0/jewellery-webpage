Our History

Three Generations of Golden Mastery

Founded in 1968 by master goldsmith Henri Monceau, Maison Dorée has remained a family atelier dedicated to the art of fine jewelry. What began as a small workshop in the heart of Paris has evolved into an internationally acclaimed brand known for impeccable craftsmanship.

Today, the third generation continues to honor traditional methods while integrating cutting-edge design paradigms, creating truly timeless family heirlooms.

Anusha Madugula
Quality Assurance

Crafted by Hand, Trusted Forever

Each Maison Dorée piece undergoes rigorous evaluation to guarantee structural integrity and gemological perfection. Our commitment to ethical luxury means preserving heritage techniques while implementing responsible sourcing initiatives.

  • Lifetime warranty protection on structure and setting.
